# Content Templates & Formulas

Proven templates for creating high-converting content across channels.

## Email Subject Lines

### Formula 1: Number + Tool/Strategy + Outcome + Timeframe
**Pattern**: "[Number] AI [Tools/Strategies] That [Specific Outcome] in [Timeframe]"

**Examples**:
- "3 AI Workflows That Cut Meeting Prep by 75% This Week"
- "5 AI Agents That Eliminate 12 Hours of Weekly Admin Tasks"
- "4 Prompt Strategies That Double Your Content Output in 30 Days"

**When to use**: Product launches, feature announcements, quick wins

### Formula 2: Peer Group + Result + Method + Case Study
**Pattern**: "How [Peer Group] [Achieve Result] Using [AI Method] (Case Study)"

**Examples**:
- "How Fortune 500 VPs Reduce Decision Time by 60% Using AI Frameworks"
- "How Sales Leaders Close 40% More Deals Using AI Agents (Real Numbers)"
- "How Product Managers Ship 3x Faster With AI Workflows"

**When to use**: Authority building, social proof, detailed stories

### Formula 3: Company + Achievement + Strategy
**Pattern**: "[Company Name] Just [Achieved Result] - Here's Their [AI Strategy]"

**Examples**:
- "Salesforce Just Saved $500K - Here's Their 3-Step AI Framework"
- "Microsoft Cut Onboarding Time 80% - Here's How They Did It"
- "Shopify Automated 90% of Support - Their AI Playbook Inside"

**When to use**: Case studies, competitive positioning, credibility

## Headlines

### Formula 1: Results-Driven with Objection Handling
**Pattern**: "How to [Achieve Specific Outcome] Using [AI Method] (Even If [Common Objection])"

**Examples**:
- "How to Reduce Decision Time by 60% Using AI Frameworks (Even If You're Non-Technical)"
- "How to Build Production AI in 30 Days (Even Without a Data Team)"
- "How to 10x Your Content Output (Even If You've Never Used AI)"

**When to use**: Landing pages, blog posts, course marketing

### Formula 2: Competitive Positioning
**Pattern**: "The [AI Strategy] Your Competitors Hope You Never Discover"

**Examples**:
- "The 3-Agent Automation System Your Competitors Hope You Never Discover"
- "The AI Framework Fortune 500s Use (But Won't Talk About)"
- "The Sales AI Strategy That's Crushing Your Competition"

**When to use**: Competitive markets, urgency creation, scarcity

### Formula 3: Transformation Promise
**Pattern**: "From [Current State] to [Desired State] in [Timeframe] Using [Method]"

**Examples**:
- "From AI-Curious to AI-Confident in 90 Days Using Proven Frameworks"
- "From Manual Processes to AI-Powered Workflows in 30 Days"
- "From Overwhelmed Executive to AI Strategy Leader in One Quarter"

**When to use**: Course launches, transformation stories, onboarding

## Value Propositions

### Master Formula
**Pattern**: "Transform from [Current State] to [Desired State] through [Method] that delivers [Specific Benefit] in [Timeframe], even if [Common Objection]."

**Examples**:
- "Transform from AI-curious executive to confident AI leader through proven frameworks that deliver measurable ROI in 90 days, even if you have zero technical background."
- "Transform from manual workflows to AI-automated systems through hands-on implementation that saves 15+ hours weekly in 30 days, even if you've never coded."
- "Transform from overwhelmed founder to AI-powered operator through strategic automation that doubles output in 60 days, even if you're a solopreneur."

**Components**:
1. **Current State**: Pain point they're experiencing now
2. **Desired State**: Aspirational outcome they want
3. **Method**: How you'll get them there (frameworks, systems, etc.)
4. **Specific Benefit**: Quantified outcome (ROI, time, revenue)
5. **Timeframe**: When they'll see results (30/60/90 days)
6. **Objection**: Remove their biggest barrier (technical knowledge, time, budget)

## Opening Lines

### Formula 1: Peer Reference + Specific Benefit
**Pattern**: "[Name] from [Recognizable Company] just shared how [they're achieving X result] with [this method]."

**Examples**:
- "Sarah from Salesforce just shared how she's saving 12 hours weekly with this AI workflow."
- "The VP of Product at Stripe just revealed their 3-step AI framework that cut research time by 80%."
- "Microsoft's Chief Strategy Officer shared how they reduced meeting time by 60% using AI agents."

**When to use**: Newsletter openings, LinkedIn posts, peer credibility

### Formula 2: Competitive Positioning + Urgency
**Pattern**: "While your competitors [debate/wait], you could be [delivering results] by [timeframe]."

**Examples**:
- "While your competitors debate AI strategy, you could be delivering measurable results by Friday."
- "While others wait for perfect, you could have 3 AI agents running by Monday."
- "While your peers research tools, you could automate your first process this week."

**When to use**: Sales emails, urgency creation, action-focused content

### Formula 3: Business Metric Hook
**Pattern**: "[Reduce/Increase X metric] by [percentage/amount] [today/this week]. This [timeframe] [method] transforms [problem] into [solution]."

**Examples**:
- "Cut your email processing time by 73% today. This 5-minute AI email setup transforms 100+ messages into 3 priority actions."
- "Reduce meeting prep by 80% this week. This simple AI workflow turns 4 hours of research into 20 minutes."
- "Double your content output in 30 days. This 3-agent system transforms blank pages into polished drafts."

**When to use**: Quick wins, tactical content, immediate value

## Call-to-Action Formulas

### Formula 1: Direct Action + Deadline + Benefit
**Pattern**: "[Action Verb] [What] by [When] to [Benefit]"

**Examples**:
- "Join the waitlist by Friday to lock in founding member pricing"
- "Book your strategy call this week to get Q1 implementation roadmap"
- "Enroll by Monday to start the cohort with 50 other executives"

### Formula 2: Question + Action
**Pattern**: "Ready to [Outcome]? [Action Verb] [What]"

**Examples**:
- "Ready to 10x your productivity? Download the AI Workflow Blueprint"
- "Ready to lead AI transformation? Reserve your cohort spot today"
- "Ready to automate your processes? Book a 15-minute demo"

### Formula 3: Scarcity + Urgency
**Pattern**: "Only [Number] [spots/seats/slots] remaining. [Action] before [Deadline]"

**Examples**:
- "Only 5 January cohort seats remaining. Enroll before Dec 31"
- "Only 3 Q1 consulting slots available. Book your discovery call this week"
- "Early access closes in 48 hours. Join now to get lifetime pricing"

## Channel-Specific Templates

### LinkedIn Post Structure
1. **Hook** (1-2 sentences, <25 words)
   - Metric or question that stops the scroll
2. **Story/Context** (2-3 sentences)
   - Peer example or personal insight
3. **Framework/Insight** (3-5 bullets)
   - Actionable takeaways
4. **CTA** (1 sentence)
   - Comment, share, or link
5. **Hashtags** (3-5)
   - #AILeadership #ExecutiveAI #BusinessTransformation

### Email Structure
1. **Subject Line** (6-8 words, outcome-focused)
2. **Opening** (1-2 sentences, hook with metric)
3. **Value Proposition** (2-3 sentences, what + why)
4. **Proof/Example** (1-2 sentences, peer success story)
5. **Implementation Steps** (3-5 bullets, tactical actions)
6. **CTA** (1 sentence, clear next step with deadline)
7. **P.S.** (Optional, additional value or urgency)

### Landing Page Sections
1. **Problem Identification** (100 words)
   - Current pain points, quantified
2. **Solution Framework** (200 words)
   - How it works, key features
3. **Proof Points** (150 words)
   - Case studies, metrics, testimonials
4. **Implementation Path** (200 words)
   - What they'll do, timeline, outcomes
5. **Risk Mitigation** (100 words)
   - Guarantees, objection handling
6. **Call to Action** (50 words)
   - Clear next step, urgency element

### Sales Outreach Sequence
**Day 1: Value Introduction**
- Open with peer reference or industry insight
- Share one relevant metric/result
- Low-friction ask (question or micro-commitment)

**Day 7: Framework Share**
- Reference previous email
- Share tactical framework/resource
- Invite response or meeting

**Day 14: Case Study**
- Show similar company's results
- Address likely objections
- Propose specific next step with date

**Day 21: Scarcity/Urgency**
- Limited availability or deadline
- Recap value proposition
- Final invitation with expiration

**Day 30: Breakup Email**
- Acknowledge no response
- Offer final resource/value
- Leave door open for future

## Formatting Best Practices

### Headlines
- 6-12 words maximum
- Include number or specific metric when possible
- Use power words: Transform, Discover, Master, Build, Accelerate
- Front-load benefit/outcome

### Bullets
- Parallel structure (all start with verb or noun)
- One idea per bullet
- Include metric/number when possible
- End with benefit, not feature

### Paragraphs
- First sentence: Key point or benefit
- Second sentence: Supporting detail or example
- Third sentence (optional): Implementation note or next step
- Maximum 2-3 sentences total

### Numbers & Metrics
- Use specific numbers: "73%" not "most"
- Include timelines: "within 30 days"
- Show contrast: "from 4 hours to 20 minutes"
- Quantify outcomes: "$500K saved" not "significant savings"
