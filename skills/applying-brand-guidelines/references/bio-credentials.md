### Extended (2-3 Paragraphs - Conference Programs, Detailed Profiles)
James Gray is a data and AI strategist with over 30 years of experience transforming how organizations leverage information to drive business outcomes. At Microsoft, he spent a decade leading global data systems that powered critical decisions across sales, marketing, customer support, and consulting organizations worldwide. His work focused on translating complex data capabilities into measurable business value for executive stakeholders.

As founder of JamesGray.AI, James now provides consulting, fractional leadership, and learning experiences that help organizations navigate AI transformation. His flagship offering, Hands-on AI for Leaders on Maven, has trained hundreds of executives and professionals to implement AI strategies that deliver ROI within 90 days. He serves as a course facilitator for UC Berkeley Haas Executive Education, where he's taught over 6,000 executives in data strategy and AI strategy programs.

James holds a BS in Electrical Engineering from Union College, an MBA from Berkeley Haas, and an MS in Information and Data Science from UC Berkeley. He is also a certified leadership and performance coach from Brown University. He brings a practitioner's perspective to AI leadership—combining technical depth with strategic business acumen and a coach's understanding of organizational change.
